Genome-wide detection of human 5’UTR variants that impact protein translation

Chaldebas M, Ponsin K, Bohlen J, Conil C, Mourelatos H, Stenson PD, Cooper DN, Abel L, Casanova JL, Cobat A#, Zhang P#. (#Equal contribution)
The American Journal of Human Genetics (AJHG), 2026.

Research Highlight: Presented as a Platform Talk at ASHG 2025. We developed 5ULTRA, a Random Forest classifier integrating SpliceAI and uORF biology to prioritize 5'UTR variants — achieving AUC 0.82 vs. 0.75 (CADD) on independent ClinVar benchmarks. Mechanistic 5'UTR Variant Scoring Expands Rare-Variant Discovery and Reveals Translational Dosage Control in the UK Biobank

Chaldebas M, Casanova JL#, Zhang P#, Cobat A#. (#Co-senior & corresponding authors)

Research Highlight: Applied 5ULTRA to 408,423 UK Biobank WGS participants across 59 quantitative traits — achieving a 1.8× improvement over CADD (74 vs. 41 genome-wide significant associations). Ablating 5ULTRA-annotated variants from CADD reduced its signal by 39%, confirming mechanistic orthogonality. Among 12 novel discoveries absent from a concurrent UKB 5'UTR catalog, NELFCD reached −log₁₀P = 49.6 for platelet count. Plasma pQTL directional concordance (Hawkes et al., 2025) validated predicted translational effects at protein level. Reveals a bidirectional translational rheostat: 5'UTR loss-of-function burden effects are quantitatively comparable to exome protein-truncating variants (r = 0.68).
